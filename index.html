Crystals Collector!
You will be given a random number to start off the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You loose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time you start the game the value of the crystals will change.
